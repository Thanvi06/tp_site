Backstory

Maroon 5 is an American pop rock band from Los Angeles, California. It currently consists of lead vocalist Adam Levine, keyboardist and rhythm guitarist Jesse Carmichael, bassist Mickey Madden, lead guitarist James Valentine, drummer Matt Flynn, keyboardist PJ Morton, and multi-instrumentalist Sam Farrar.The band members met in high school and were orginally names themselves, Kara's Flowers, later on changing it to the name most of us now know Maroon 5!

The Journey

The band met in 1994 in high school and slowly became closer and closer based off their love for music and creating music. They realeased their first single " We like digging", while in high school. Shortly after, they were signed to Reprise Records and realeased their first album, The Fourth World. Soon after the album released, the record company dropped them as the memebers went to college. In 2001, the band reuninted and changed the name to "Maroon 5". On June,25,2002 they realeased their debut album with Octone Records, Songs about Jane. The bands second album, " It wont be soon long before", realeased in may of 2007. It became number 1 on the billboard 200 charts, and the lead single became the bands first number one song. The band countinued to release albums with different record companies for several years. Many of their songs charted number 1 on the billboad charts. Some including: Make me Wonder, Moves like Jagger, One more night, Girls like You, etc. The bands style tends to change, album to album.

Where are they now?

Maroon 5 has gained over 26 million youtube subscribers since creating their channel in 2006. They have a cummulative views of over 13 billion. THe band now has a total of 6 studio albums and 3 live albums. The band has 6 number one songs, and over 15 top 10 songs. They hvae recieved 3 grammy awards , 2 american music awards, 5 teen choice awards, and 8 billboard music award. Maroon 5 has performed at many live events including award shows, the voice, and the 2019 superbowl halftime show.
